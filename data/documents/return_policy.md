# Return Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we make returns easy.

## Return Window
- **Standard Returns**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Extended Returns**: 60 days for gift purchases (with proof of gift receipt)
- **Final Sale Items**: Non-returnable (marked at checkout)

## Return Conditions
Items must be:
- Unused and in original condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- Accompanied by the original receipt or packing slip

## How to Return
1. Contact support at support@company.com or call 1-800-RETURNS
2. Provide your order number
3. You'll receive a prepaid return shipping label
4. Ship the item back to us within 7 days

## Refund Timeline
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable
- Return shipping is free (we provide a prepaid label)

## Exceptions
- Electronics: 14-day return window
- Customized products: Non-returnable
- Items purchased during final sale events: Non-returnable
- Clearance items (marked red): 14-day return window

## Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
- Contact us within 24 hours with photos
- We'll replace the item or issue a full refund
- Free return shipping and replacement shipping provided
